About Us

Founded in 2009, Aditya College has grown to become a distinguished institution renowned for its unwavering commitment to academic excellence and innovation. Our mission is clear: to empower students with the knowledge and skills to excel in a rapidly changing world. We nurture critical thinking, creativity, and a passion for lifelong learning.

With a vision of global recognition, Aditya College aspires to be a catalyst for positive change through education, research, and community engagement. Our core values include a dedication to excellence, a celebration of diversity, a commitment to innovation, and the formation of a strong and inclusive community.

Nestled in the heart of Madanapalle, our campus offers an inspiring environment for academic exploration and personal growth. Join us at Aditya College of Engineering and be part of a dynamic community that embraces limitless possibilities and shapes the leaders of tomorrow.

Admissions

Welcome to the gateway of opportunity! At Aditya, we are dedicated to helping you turn your dreams into reality. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, accessible, and supportive of your aspirations.

Requirements

Our admission requirements vary by program and level of study. Whether you're a first-time undergraduate or a graduate student seeking to advance your education, our admissions team is here to guide you through the process.

How to Apply

1.Online Application: Begin your journey by filling out our online application form, providing the necessary information and documents.


2.Application Fee: Pay the application fee through our secure portal. Fee waivers may be available for eligible candidates.


3.Transcripts and Documents: Submit your academic transcripts, standardized test scores (if applicable), and any additional required documents.


4.Letters of Recommendation: Secure letters of recommendation from educators, mentors, or employers who can speak to your qualifications and potential.


5.Interviews and Portfolios: Some programs may require interviews or portfolios as part of the application process. Be sure to check specific program requirements.


6.Financial Aid: Explore scholarship and financial aid opportunities to help make your education affordable.
